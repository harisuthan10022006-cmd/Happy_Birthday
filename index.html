For You, Jannu 💙

Hii ma, ennaku engha start pannurathune theriyala — but first of all oru thanks solli itha start pannanum nu nenaikuren. Cause across these years nee eppovum ennakagha enna motivate pannitu oru confidence boost kuduthu irruka… atharkagha oru periya nandriiii 🙏💙

Itha unta romba naala sollanum nu irrunthen. Antha three/four years gap appuram meet pannunom la — appo "I was like getting back home from my grave." Appuram eppovum pola text pannitu athe connection oda pesitu irrunthom, irrukom and eppovum appudiye irrupom.

And yes — naan ithu vara yaroda msgkum avalolam wait panna maaten, but ennamo theriyala… unta irrunthu oru single msg varathanu avalo wait pannuven. Theriyala ethuku nu keekathe, ennakum theriyala 😅💙

Nee ithu vara yaariayum thitti paathathu illa — but nee enna thittum bothu athu ennaku konjam funnaa irrukum. En parents and teachers appuram naan athighama thittu vaanghunathum unta than, and athighama enjoy pannunathu nee thitturatha than. "Vekkame illaiyada" nu keetratha — nee thittum bothu vekkam, maanam ellam rekka katti paranthu poidum 😂

And oru naal keetila — ethuku enta ellathaiyum sollitu irrukanu? Ethuku sollurena… athu unta enna nadanthuchu nu sollala na annaiku day mulusa mudincha maari irrukathu. And unna vitta naan solluratha ellam ketka yaaru irruka? 😅 Athan ellathiyum unta sollitu irruken — and nee enna sonnalum atha continue pannuven. So athukaga ippove mannipu keetukolghiren… mannichuuu 🙏😁

Innum neraiya sollanum nu thonuthu — but ellam oru naal meet pannurappo solluren, illa call pannurappo solluren. Ithudan unghalidamirunthu vidai peruvathu — unghalin naan. 💙🎻
